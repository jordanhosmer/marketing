LawPal is the best way for lawyers to stay on top of every project.
Better tools and better communication make for better lawyers and happier clients.

Why
The best lawyers going forward will be lean, doing away with unnecessary costs and cutting out inefficiencies. Smaller teams will do more work in less time and technology will play a key role in this transition. Much of what good lawyers do cannot be replicated by computer systems. But most of what good lawyers do can be done more efficiently and effectively through appropriate automation.

How
LawPal is a collaborative platform which helps lawyers work with their clients to deliver legal services in a less costly, more transparent and client-friendly way. Our interactive checklist allows you to structure your deal, focus your team and watch the transaction progress in real time. Never ask “Who’s doing what?” or "Where is that document?" again.
