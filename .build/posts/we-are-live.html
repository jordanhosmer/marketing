We are live!

LawPal officially launches at the 2014 ABA TechShow in Chicago


We are excited to announce the launch of our collaborative platform, which went live Thursday March 27, 2014 at the ABA TechShow in Chicago.

The platform is a cloud-based workspace for planning, executing and tracking legal work, allowing lawyers and clients centralized access to all documents, communications and notes. Check out Bob Ambrogi’s recap of the show and his review of our “simple and — dare I say it — elegant” solution. More Here.

We also want to give a special shoutout to our friends in the startup world who also made the trip to the ABA TechShow: Jake Heller from CaseText, Nikhil Nirmel from LawDingo, Andy Wilson from Logikcull and Matthew Tollin from WireLawyer. Always great to see you guys!

Go to lawpal.com and give it a spin. If you have any questions please contact me at yael@lawpal.com. I am here to make sure that you absolutely love using the platform. We look forward to your feedback.

Enjoy!

Yael Citro, Alex Halliday and Ross Crawford — Founders of LawPal
